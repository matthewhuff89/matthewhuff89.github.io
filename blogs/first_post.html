Awareness

July 11, 2016

Return to Main Page

It's strange.

I've realized today, that pretty much everything in my life that I've ever considered a problem, usually arose through a lack of awareness.

For instance, fucking my money up. Spending more than I could. Not saving enough and then getting hit with an emergency...if I had simply paid attention to my spending, to my saving, it would have never become a problem.

How often does something you're actively paying attention to become a problem in your life?

I find it to be a rare occurence honestly.

I mean, sure. Maybe in video games, like a match of Street Fighter, and someone just turns up the heat and whoops your ass...but even then. That just suggests a lack of awareness about techniques, positioning, and so many more than things that you can improve on.

It's oddly comforting.

"

It's making it harder for me to think of things as "mistakes" now.

I just see opportunities to be more aware now.

-- Matt
