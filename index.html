Purpose

Hey, I'm Matt.

I'm a very purpose, and principle-minded person.

After my experiences at college and beyond, I realized that for me to live a truly dope, happy, present life, I needed to be clear with my intentions.

A powerful, singular purpose can bestow great power and motivate one to act in ways they previously thought they were incapable of.

It can be a guiding light in the darkest of times.

After spending months in meditation and reflection after a private mental breakdown...I created my own purpose. My own way of life.

My purpose in life, is to master my mind, body, and spirit, and help others do the same with technology and imagination.

That's it. Everything I do is to stay true to that idea.

Of course, it's not some immutable, unchanging thing, but...for the moment, this feels right, y'know?

What's your purpose? How do you choose to live your life?
